FAQs

How do I know my investment is safe?

Your investment is protected by a legal agreement signed on a ₹100 stamp paper. This legally binding contract guarantees the terms of the offer and ensures that your ₹15,000 is secure.

What do I get when I invest ₹15,000?

When you invest ₹15,000, you will receive 11 grocery coupons, each worth ₹4,000, to be used once a month. If you don’t use the full amount of a coupon in a given month, we will reimburse the remaining balance in cash.

What happens if I don't use the full ₹4,000 coupon in a month?

If you don’t manage to use the full ₹4,000 in a particular month, the remaining balance will be credited to you in cash, so you never lose money.

What kind of legal protection do I get?

Each participant signs a legal agreement on a ₹100 stamp paper. This contract outlines the terms of the investment and ensures your money is protected. (Note: You can download the Agreement Sample from the above section)

What if the price of groceries increases?

Regardless of price changes or inflation, your coupon value remains ₹4,000 each month, ensuring you always get a stable and predictable amount for your groceries.

Is there a limit to how many times I can invest?

At this time, each customer is allowed one active investment per household. This ensures fair access to the program for all participants.

How do I receive the cashback if I don’t use the full coupon amount?

The cashback amount will be provided to you via google pay, phonepe or any desired UPI method you like, at the time of purchase of grocery. You can also collect the Cash from our grocery outlet.
